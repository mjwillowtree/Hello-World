# Post-2025 529->ABLE Rollover Status Memo

Updated: 2026-03-10  
Confidence: High (Tier 1 primary law + current codification notes)

## Bottom line

The old sunset phrase for 529->ABLE rollovers is no longer in controlling law.

- Pub. L. 119-21, section 70117(a), amended IRC section 529(c)(3)(C)(i)(III) by striking "before January 1, 2026,".
- Pub. L. 119-21, section 70115(a)(2), amended IRC section 529A(b)(2)(B)(ii) by striking "before January 1, 2026".

As a result, older summaries that still quote the pre-2026 sunset are historical and should not be treated as controlling.

## Advisor-safe language

"Current federal law reflects 2025 amendments that removed the prior pre-2026 sunset text from IRC sections 529 and 529A. A 529-to-ABLE rollover can be federally qualified if all other statutory conditions are met (same beneficiary or qualifying family member relationship and contribution-limit constraints)."

## Conservative fallback language (if any source conflict appears)

"If a publication or webpage appears to retain pre-2026 sunset wording, rely on the amended statute and current codification notes first; treat older summaries as historical until updated."
